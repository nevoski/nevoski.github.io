Hello!

My name is Nicholas, and I use UX design to help you communicate your message clearly and beautifully.

Here's a brief overview of my process:

Research

Ideation & Testing

Wireframes

Mockups

Competitive analyses, surveys, and user interviews help me get to the root of the design challenge.

After learning about the problem, I sketch my initial ideas and conduct the first round of user testing.

After sketching and testing initial ideas, I build higher fidelity wireframes and test some more!

Finally, I build high fidelity mockups using rapid prototyping software, as well as html and css.
