Hello!

My name is Nicholas, and I use UX design to help you communicate your message clearly and beautifully.

Here's a brief overview of my process:

Research

Competitive analyses, surveys, and user interviews are some of the techniques that help me get to the root of the design challenge.

Sketching, Wireframing, & Testing

Then, I sketch my initial ideas and seek out some preliminary feedback. After sketching, I build higher fidelity wireframes and conduct multiple rounds of usability testing to refine the design. This stage of the process is flexible, and depends on the contraints and scope of the project.

Mockups

Finally, I build high fidelity mockups using illustration software, rapid prototyping software, HTML, CSS, and jQuery.
